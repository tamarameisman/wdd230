Target Audience and Scenarios

Jane Jones

21 years old - 15+ year resident - New to the area - Single - Needs friends - Needs apartment - Loves hiking and fishing

Teens and Young Adults are a major user group for my website. They will go to the Community Now Website to connect with others in their area. They are an active, creative, and transient group that will want to now what is happening now and in the near furture in there community. A few of the searches/questions they will have are:

  • What is the weather this weekend (So, I know how to dress for my hot date)?
  • When is the next concert in Soda Springs?
  • Where is the best singles meeting places in Preston?
  • What are good hiking and biking trails in Fish Haven?
  • What short term apartments are available to rent?

Shaun Smith

47 years old - 15+ year resident - Married with 4 teenaged kids - Wants great neighborhood and schools - Wants family bonding activities - Needs to sell his truck

Parents of all ages are another major user group for my website. They will go to the Community Now Website to find family friend activities, to buy and sell things, local new and weather. They are looking to build there community and grow roots for the furture. A few of the searches/questions they will have are:

  • What is the weather today (So, I know if my kid needs a jacket at the bus stop)?
  • When is the next school board meeting?
  • Where do I vote?
  • What is the calender of my local high football games?
  • What local service projects can my family participate in?
