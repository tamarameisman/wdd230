Site Purpose

Community Now is a site that provides Prestion, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven, Idaho with up to the date weather conditions and other town information or relevant articles/content. It will give the user knowledge to better plan their day, week, or month. It will provide them with everything going on in there area and how it affects them. This will empower them to get the most out of their community now.
